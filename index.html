Who are we?

Our Organization

The UST Computer Science Society (UST-CSS) is the official academic organization of the Department of Computer Science at the University of Santo Tomas. Our mission is to foster a community of learners and innovators who are passionate about technology and its applications. We aim to provide our members with opportunities for personal and professional growth through various activities, workshops, and events.

What We Do

Workshops and Seminars

We organize workshops and seminars on the latest technologies and trends in computer science.

Competitions

We participate in and host coding competitions, hackathons, and other tech-related contests.

Community Outreach

We conduct outreach programs to share our knowledge and skills with the wider community.

Networking Events

We provide opportunities for our members to network with industry professionals and alumni.

Project Development

We encourage our members to work on projects that solve real-world problems and showcase their skills.
